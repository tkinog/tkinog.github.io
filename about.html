About Me

My name is Tom, and I am a 24 years old flight instructor in the San Fernano Valley area.
I took my first discovery flight in the summer of 2017, and was immedetialy hooked. I worked through my private, instrument, and commercial certificates (airplane single-engine, land) and eventually got my instructor license in Novermber 2019. I have been instructing ever since. I have also added instrument-instructor rating, and comercial multi-engine rating.


I am currently working full-time as a flight instructor and also attending CSUN looking to receive a degree in computer science. In the future, I hope to become an airline pilot, but in the meantime, I look to improve myself as a pilot and as an instructor, and I aim to share my skills and knowledge with my students and to those who possess the same passion as me.
